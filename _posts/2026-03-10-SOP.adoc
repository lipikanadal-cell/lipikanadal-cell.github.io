= Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for Vehicle Intake

*Department*: Repair Operations

*Applies To*: Service Advisors, Estimators, Intake Technicians

*Objective*: Ensure accurate, safe, and consistent intake of vehicles for collision repair while maintaining quality standards, documentation accuracy, and customer satisfaction.

== Step 1: Customer Check-In
1.	Confirm customer identity.
2.	Verify appointment in the system.
3.	Collect the following information:

* Customer contact information
* Insurance company and claim number
* Accident description
* Date of incident
4.	Review repair authorization and obtain customer signature.
 
== Step 2: Initial Vehicle Inspection
1. Conduct a walkaround with the customer when possible.

2. Document the following:

* Exterior damage
* Existing scratches or dents	
* Wheel condition
* Glass damage
* Interior condition
* Missing components

[NOTE]
Document pre-existing damage clearly.

== Step 3: Photo Documentation
1. Capture clear photos of:

* Front view
* Rear view
* Left side
* Right side
* VIN plate
* Odometer
* Interior dashboard
* All damaged areas
* Wheel condition

[NOTE]
Ensure photos are uploaded to the management system.
 
== Step 4: Vehicle Information Verification
1. Verify and record:

* VIN
* License plate
* Vehicle model and trim
* Mileage
* Battery level
* Key or key card availability

[NOTE]
Confirm vehicle matches repair order.

== Step 5: Functional Check
1. Check and document:

* Drivability
* Warning lights
* Screen functionality
* Door operation
* Camera condition
* Wheel alignment symptoms

[NOTE]
If vehicle is not drivable, mark as *Non-Operational*.
 
== Step 6: Personal Belongings

1. Request customer to remove personal items.
2. If items remain:

* Document them
* Store securely if necessary
* Note in intake report

[NOTE]
Mention that the Collision Center is not responsible for personal belongings left in the vehicle.
 
== Step 7: Insurance Documentation
1. Confirm:

* Claim number
* Date of incident
* Insurance adjuster contact

[NOTE]
Upload any insurance documents to the repair file.
 
== Step 8: Create Repair Order
1. Generate a repair order including:

* Customer details
* Vehicle details
* Reported damage
* Initial estimate
* Intake photos
* Authorization signatures

[NOTE]
Provide customer with copy.
 
== Step 9: Customer Communication

1. Before customer leaves, explain:

•	Next steps in repair process
•	Estimated timeline
•	Insurance coordination
•	Communication method (phone / email)

[NOTE]
Provide contact information for the service center.
